It's time to face your fears.

Are you brave enough to face the demons that lurk in the shadows? In this heart-pumping horror survival game, available now on Steam, you'll play as Lilith, an outcast with a fascination for the occult and a history of terrifying nightmares. When Lilith stumbles upon a mysterious artefact, she's transported to a hellish landscape where humanoid demons hunt humans for the thrill of fear. Solve puzzles, uncover dark secrets, and outwit the demons in intense chase scenes that will have you on the edge of your seat. Will she find a way home, or make a new home in this strange land?

Explore the World of Nefaryous

Nefaryous is a game that delves deep into the world of demons and the unknown, but what sets it apart is the depth of its characters. The game boasts a diverse cast of characters, each with their own intriguing past and motives. From the six main demons who rule over the demon dimension to Lily, the outcast who accidentally finds herself transported there, each character is well-rounded and unique.

As you navigate through the game, you will encounter demons that are far from one-dimensional. They each have a nefaryous past and are driven by their own evil intentions. However, as you get to know them, you will realize that their motivations are more complex than just pure malevolence. The game allows you to interact with the demons, learn about their backstories, and uncover their true intentions.

"Are you brave enough to face the demons that lurk in the shadows?"

In Nefaryous, you must navigate a demon-infested world while hiding your fear and maintaining a calm demeanor. You'll have to live among the demons, learn their habits, and avoid detection if you want to survive. But sometimes, you'll have no choice but to run, with demons hot on your heels.

The world of Nefaryous is filled with haunting and mesmerizing POIs that will leave you breathless. Explore the gothic castle, with its towering spires and ominous presence, where danger lurks around every corner. Dare to venture into the never-ending tunnels of the crypts, where you'll face your deepest fears and experience true terror.

Be mesmerized by the endless forest with its bright pink night sky, where the trees are tall and twisted, and the secrets hidden within are waiting to be discovered. Finally, take a plunge into the deep black lagoon that surrounds the castle, where the waters are murky and treacherous, and the creatures that lurk below are waiting to strike.
