Characters

Doctor William Hills

William Hilith, also known as “Doc”, was a physician during the bubonic plague in the 1300s. He was skilled in treating infected patients, but his true fascination lay in scientific experiments. His patients were subjected to painful experimental procedures, and after they died, he would take their bodies to perform autopsies and various experiments on them. Eventually, he decided to not treat the patients at all, but rather administer poison to speed up the disease and then experiment on their bodies. He was found guilty of killing over 200 patients and sentenced to death. He was burned at the stake in 1349, but his soul lived on as a demon in the crypts under the castle.

As a demon, Doc keeps his appearance as a plague doctor. He wears a black robe and a mask with glass eyes and a beak, sewn roughly together. He is a short and lanky demon with multiple small and twisted horns growing out of his head and beak. Half of his mask is ripped off, revealing his grotesque face with dark and hollow features. He possesses bubonic flames, a fire power that he controls from the palms of his hands. When the flames touch the skin, black warts form all over the body, and the disease kills the victim from the inside out. He collects ingredients for his "remedies" outside the castle walls and experiments on the bodies of his victims after each kill. Doc is a dangerous demon, always on the hunt for new specimens to experiment on, and he will stop at nothing to satisfy his curiosity.
