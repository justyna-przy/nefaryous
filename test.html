Escape this nightmare.

Meet Lily, a mortally confused outcast with a dark fascination for the occult and an unsettling history of terrifying nightmares. One fateful day, Lilith discovers a mysterious artefact in an antique shop and finds herself transported to a hellish landscape of pink skies and a looming gothic castle. But this is no ordinary dream - the demons that inhabit this world are real, and they hunt humans for the thrill of fear. With Lilith's every move watched and judged by the sinister creatures, she must control her fear and master her surroundings to survive. Explore the castle, forest and crypts, solve twisted puzzles, and brace yourself for intense chase scenes as you fight to uncover the truth about Lilith's new reality. Will she find a way home, or will she be forever trapped in this realm of nightmares?

About the Game and 22Studios

22 Studios is an independent game development studio based in Sacramento! Our small team is dedicated to creating fun and innovative games for our community to enjoy. We're famous for our hit horror game Nefaryous, but we've also developed smaller games such as Battlesaga and Lulu. We appreciate our fans' support and are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Here on our website, you can find updates on our latest projects in our development blog, where we share insights on the game development process and behind-the-scenes content, or read our blog for industry insights and commentary. Get to know our team and their unique perspectives.

Nefaryous Development Process

Nefaryous began development in early 2021 with a small team of passionate game developers. The concept was initially inspired by various horror and dark fantasy games, and the team spent countless hours brainstorming and refining the game's mechanics, story, and characters. As development progressed, the team received positive feedback from playtesters and fans, which further motivated them to create an engaging and immersive experience. With a commitment to quality and a passion for the horror genre, Nefaryous slowly took shape and evolved into the game it is today.
